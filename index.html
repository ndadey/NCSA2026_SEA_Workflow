NCSA 2026 Workshop

Building Robust, Reproducible State Analytic Workflows: From Vision to Practice

Center for Assessment · Arizona Department of Education · Montana Office of Public Instruction

In an ideal world, state education agency staff would carry out assessment and accountability analysis and reporting within robust, reproducible workflows — work that is well built, well documented, and maintained from year to year. This workshop offers pragmatic, incremental approaches for closing the gap between that vision and current practice, drawing on case studies from Arizona and Montana and a parallel open-access eBook of code, guidance, and adaptable templates. The slides and resources below are available to revisit after the session.
